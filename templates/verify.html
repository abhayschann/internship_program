Certificate Verification

Enter the certificate ID to verify its authenticity. You can find the certificate ID on the certificate itself or by scanning the QR code.

How to Verify a Certificate

1
Find the Certificate ID

Locate the Certificate ID at the bottom of the certificate.

2
Enter the ID Above

Type the Certificate ID in the form above and click "Verify Certificate".

3
Alternative: Scan QR Code

Scan the QR code on the certificate to be redirected to the verification page.

4
View Certificate Details

If the certificate is valid, you'll see all the details of the certificate.
